FAQ
  • What are the system requirements?

    macOS 13 Ventura or later, on Apple Silicon or Intel. Claude Code (claude CLI) must be installed.

  • Which terminals are supported?

    iTerm2, macOS Terminal, and Warp are natively supported. Sessions launched from IDE integrated terminals — VS Code, Cursor, Android Studio, and more — are also detected automatically.

  • How does AgentManager detect sessions?

    AgentManager uses Claude Code hooks to detect sessions. During onboarding, a hook is registered automatically — after that, every claude session you start appears in the list with no further setup.

  • Where does my session data go?

    Nowhere. AgentManager runs entirely on your Mac and does not send session data to any external server. Your sessions stay on your machine.

  • Is there a free trial? How is pricing structured?

    Yes — a 7-day free trial (coming soon) with all features unlocked, no credit card required. After the trial: Monthly at $4.99/mo, or Annual at $48/yr (~2 months free).

  • How many Macs can I use one license on?

    There is no limit on the number of Macs per license. Install and activate AgentManager on as many machines as you like.
